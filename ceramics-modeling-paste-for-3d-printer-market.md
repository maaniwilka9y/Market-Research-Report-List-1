<p><p>Ceramics modeling paste for 3D printing is categorized into two main types: low viscosity and high viscosity. Low viscosity pastes are fluid and easier to extrude, making them suitable for intricate designs and fine details. They allow for smoother layer application, ideal for complex geometries. In contrast, high viscosity pastes are thicker and provide greater structural stability, making them ideal for larger, more robust prints. Each type serves different applications, catering to varied needs within the ceramics printing market.</p></p>
