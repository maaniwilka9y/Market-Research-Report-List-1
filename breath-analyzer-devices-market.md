<p><strong>The Breath Analyzer Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fuel Cell Breath Analyzers</li><li>Semiconductor Breath Analyzers</li><li>Infrared Breath Analyzers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Breath analyzer devices are categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Fuel Cell Breath Analyzers** utilize electrochemical reactions to measure alcohol content in breath, known for their accuracy and quick results. </p><p>2. **Semiconductor Breath Analyzers** employ metal-oxide sensors, offering a cost-effective option but with less precision compared to fuel cells. </p><p>3. **Infrared Breath Analyzers** use infrared spectroscopy to detect gases, providing high sensitivity and the ability to measure various compounds, making them suitable for medical diagnostics.</p></p>
