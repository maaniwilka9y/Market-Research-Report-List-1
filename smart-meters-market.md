<p><p>The smart meter market is segmented into single-phase and three-phase smart meters. Single-phase smart meters are primarily used in residential applications, measuring electricity consumption for homes and small businesses. They are simpler in design and suitable for lower energy demands. In contrast, three-phase smart meters cater to industrial and commercial sectors, measuring higher energy usage across three phases of electricity supply. This distinction allows for tailored solutions addressing varying energy consumption needs and enhancing grid management efficiency.</p></p>
