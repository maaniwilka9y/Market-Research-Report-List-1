<p><p>A Surface Solar Pump is a solar-powered water pumping system designed to lift water from a surface source such as a well or a reservoir. Primarily used in agricultural irrigation, livestock watering, and residential applications, these pumps leverage solar energy to operate, offering a renewable and eco-friendly alternative to traditional diesel-powered pumps. The growing emphasis on sustainable farming practices and the need for efficient water management are driving the adoption of surface solar pumps.</p><p>The Surface Solar Pump Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.3% during the forecast period.
