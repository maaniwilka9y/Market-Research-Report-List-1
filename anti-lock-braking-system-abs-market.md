<p><p>The Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) market includes key components such as the Electronic Control Unit (ECU), sensors, and hydraulic units. The ECU manages the braking system by processing data from the sensors that monitor wheel speed and traction. Sensors detect wheel lock-up and relay information to the ECU, which adjusts brake pressure accordingly. The hydraulic unit modulates brake pressure during skidding, ensuring optimal traction and control, enhancing vehicle safety and performance across various automotive applications.</p></p>
