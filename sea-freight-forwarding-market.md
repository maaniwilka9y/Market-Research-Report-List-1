<p><ul><li>Full Container Load (FCL)</li><li>Less-Than Container Load (LCL)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sea freight forwarding encompasses various market types tailored to shipping needs. Full Container Load (FCL) involves shipping an entire container, ideal for large shipments, offering cost efficiency and faster delivery. Less-Than Container Load (LCL) consolidates cargo from multiple shippers into a single container, suitable for smaller shipments, though delivery may take longer due to handling. Other markets include breakbulk, where individual items are loaded directly into the vessel, and roll-on/roll-off (RoRo) for vehicles, catering to diverse shipping requirements.</p></p>
