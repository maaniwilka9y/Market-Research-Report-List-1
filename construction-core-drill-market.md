<p><p>The construction core drill market primarily features two types of drill bits: wet and dry. Wet drill bits utilize water to cool the bit and reduce dust, making them ideal for applications where dust control and bit longevity are crucial. In contrast, dry drill bits operate without water and are usually employed in less challenging materials or where groundwater is a concern. Both types cater to various construction needs, emphasizing efficiency and adaptability in drilling operations.</p></p>
