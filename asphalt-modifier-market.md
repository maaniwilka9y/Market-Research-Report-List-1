Chemical modifiers involve additives that interact chemically with asphalt to improve performance characteristics, like stability and durability. Other modifiers encompass a range of innovative products and technologies, including bio-based options or waste materials, aimed at enhancing the overall performance and sustainability of asphalt mixtures.</p></p>
