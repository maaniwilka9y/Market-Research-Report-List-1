<p><p>The alkaline proteases market is primarily segmented into two types: serine proteases and metalloproteases. Serine proteases employ a serine residue for catalysis and play vital roles in various industrial applications, including food processing and detergents. Metalloproteases, on the other hand, utilize metal ions for enzymatic activity and are commonly used in pharmaceuticals and biotechnological processes. Both types of proteases are essential for breaking down proteins, enhancing product performance, and catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
