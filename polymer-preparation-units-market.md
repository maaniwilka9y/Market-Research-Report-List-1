<p><ul><li>Powder or Granular Polymer Preparation Units</li><li>Liquid Polymer Preparation Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polymer preparation units can be categorized into two main types based on their physical forms: powder or granular polymer preparation units and liquid polymer preparation units. Powder or granular units are designed to handle solid polymer materials, facilitating processes like mixing, blending, and granulation. In contrast, liquid polymer preparation units are tailored for the processing and handling of polymers in liquid form, enabling reactions and formulations that require a fluid medium. Each type serves distinct applications in various industries, optimizing polymer production techniques.</p></p>
