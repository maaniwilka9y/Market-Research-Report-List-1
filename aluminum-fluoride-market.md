<p><p>The Aluminum Fluoride market is categorized into three primary types: Dry AlF3, Anhydrous AlF3, and Wet AlF3. Dry AlF3 is a powdered form, commonly used in aluminum production for improving electrolytic processes. Anhydrous AlF3 is a pure, moisture-free variant, utilized in chemical synthesis and specialized applications. Wet AlF3 contains water and is often employed in aluminum smelting processes. Each type serves specific industrial needs, influencing production techniques and end-use applications in various sectors.</p></p>
