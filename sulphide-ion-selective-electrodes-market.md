<p><p>The sulfide ion selective electrodes market is primarily categorized into two main types: crystal membrane and PVC membrane electrodes. Crystal membrane electrodes utilize a solid crystalline structure that selectively interacts with sulfide ions, offering high stability and selectivity. In contrast, PVC membrane electrodes are constructed with a polymer-based membrane containing specific ionophores to detect sulfide ions. Both types cater to various applications in environmental monitoring, wastewater treatment, and industrial processes, enabling precise sulfide ion measurement and analysis.</p></p>
