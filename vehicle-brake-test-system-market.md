<p><ul><li>Roller Brake Test Machine</li><li>Plate Brake Test Machine</li><li>Decelerometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Brake Test System market comprises three primary types: Roller Brake Test Machines, Plate Brake Test Machines, and Decelerometers. </p><p>Roller Brake Test Machines assess brake performance by simulating real-world driving conditions, using rollers to measure brake force. Plate Brake Test Machines utilize a flat surface to evaluate braking efficiency, often in-service environments. Decelerometers, on the other hand, are portable devices that measure vehicle deceleration, providing critical data for evaluating brake system effectiveness. Each type serves distinct testing needs for ensuring vehicle safety and compliance.</p></p>
