<p><p>Offshore drilling rigs are essential for extracting oil and gas from beneath the seabed. Jackup rigs are mobile platforms supported by long legs, ideal for shallow waters. Semi-submersible rigs float on the surface, anchored by mooring lines, and are suited for deeper waters due to their stability in rough sea conditions. Drillships are specially designed vessels that incorporate drilling equipment and are equipped for deepwater exploration, offering high mobility and advanced technology for offshore drilling operations.</p></p>
