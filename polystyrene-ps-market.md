<p><p>Polystyrene (PS) is a versatile thermoplastic used in various applications. The market is primarily segmented into three types: Expanded Polystyrene (EPS), which is lightweight and commonly used in packaging and insulation; High-Impact Polystyrene (HIPS), known for its durability and impact resistance, often used in consumer goods and appliances; and Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene (SPS), a block copolymer that combines flexibility and strength, ideal for automotive and industrial applications. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to different industry needs.</p></p>
