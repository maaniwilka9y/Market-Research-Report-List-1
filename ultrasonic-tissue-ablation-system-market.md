<p><p>The Ultrasonic Tissue Ablation System market primarily includes High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) and Magnetic Resonance-guided Focused Ultrasound (MRgFUS). HIFU uses concentrated ultrasound waves to thermally destroy tissue, commonly applied in treating tumors and uterine fibroids. MRgFUS combines real-time imaging with ultrasound guidance, allowing for precise targeting of tissues, reducing damage to surrounding areas. Both technologies emphasize minimally invasive procedures, enhancing patient safety and recovery, while effectively treating various medical conditions.</p></p>
