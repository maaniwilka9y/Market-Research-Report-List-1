<p><ul><li>Straight Rotary Air Gate Valve</li><li>Side Entry Rotary Air Gate Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary air gate valve market features two main types: straight rotary air gate valves and side entry rotary air gate valves. Straight rotary air gate valves operate by rotating a disk in-line with the flow, providing efficient and reliable control of airflow. In contrast, side entry rotary air gate valves are designed to facilitate side connections, allowing for easier installation and maintenance in compact spaces. Both types are essential in various industrial applications for regulating air and material flow effectively.</p></p>
