<p><ul><li>Latex</li><li>Nitrile Rubber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical gloves market comprises primarily latex and nitrile rubber types. Latex gloves, made from natural rubber, offer excellent elasticity and comfort, making them suitable for various medical procedures. However, they can cause allergic reactions in some users. Nitrile gloves, constructed from synthetic rubber, are resistant to punctures and chemicals, providing a safer alternative for those with latex allergies. They are increasingly popular in healthcare settings due to their durability and barrier protection, driving market growth.</p></p>
