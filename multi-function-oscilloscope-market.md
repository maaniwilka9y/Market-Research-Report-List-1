<p><p>The multi-function oscilloscope market comprises two main types: digital oscilloscopes and analog oscilloscopes. Digital oscilloscopes utilize advanced sampling techniques to capture and display waveforms, offering greater accuracy, storage, and analysis capabilities. They are widely used in modern electronics troubleshooting and research. In contrast, analog oscilloscopes display waveforms directly in a continuous manner, providing real-time visual feedback. Although less common in contemporary applications, they are still utilized for specific tasks requiring immediate waveform observation, especially in educational settings.</p></p>
