<p><p>The medical suction regulators market features two primary types: continuous suction regulators and intermittent suction regulators. Continuous suction regulators provide a constant vacuum necessary for procedures requiring steady fluid removal, ensuring a continuous flow for effective suction. In contrast, intermittent suction regulators deliver suction in cycles, allowing for periods of suction followed by release, ideal for applications that require controlled drainage. Both types play a crucial role in medical settings, enhancing patient care by facilitating the removal of fluids and obstructions.</p></p>
