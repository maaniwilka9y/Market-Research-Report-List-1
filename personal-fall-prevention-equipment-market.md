<p><p>The Personal Fall Prevention Equipment market consists of several essential types aimed at enhancing worker safety at heights. Safety harnesses secure individuals by distributing fall forces across the body. Safety lanyards connect harnesses to anchor points, allowing for movement while preventing falls. Anchorage connectors attach safety equipment to stable structures. Fall arresters stop falls in progress, minimizing injury risk. Lifeline systems provide an extended connection for workers, facilitating mobility. Other products include additional safety gear and accessories, enhancing overall fall protection.</p></p>
