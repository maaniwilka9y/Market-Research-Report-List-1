<p><p>The ceramic substrate market consists of various types, primarily including DBC (Direct Bonded Copper), which features a copper layer bonded directly to the ceramic for efficient heat dissipation. DPC (Dual-Sided Polished Ceramic) substrates provide electrical insulation and thermal conductance while supporting high-density interconnections. AMB (Active Metal Brazed) substrates utilize a brazing process for improved mechanical strength and thermal performance. Other substrates may encompass diverse materials and manufacturing techniques tailored for specific applications in electronics and power devices.</p></p>
