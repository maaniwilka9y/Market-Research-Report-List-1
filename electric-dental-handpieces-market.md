<p><strong>The Electric Dental Handpieces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-Speed Electric Dental Handpieces</li><li>Low-Speed Electric Dental Handpieces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric dental handpieces market comprises two primary types: high-speed and low-speed electric dental handpieces. High-speed handpieces are designed for rapid cutting and crown preparation, often operating at speeds exceeding 300,000 RPM, making them ideal for intricate procedures. In contrast, low-speed handpieces, operating between 5,000 to 40,000 RPM, are utilized for tasks like polishing, finishing, and restorative work, providing greater control for delicate procedures. Both types enhance efficiency, precision, and patient comfort in dental practices.</p></p>
