<p><p>Weak Acid Cation Exchange Resins (WAC) are specialized materials used for water treatment and purification processes. The two primary market types are Gel WAC and Macroporous WAC. Gel Weak Acid Cation Exchange Resins offer high capacity and stability but may have slower kinetics, making them suitable for applications with low flow rates. In contrast, Macroporous WAC resins provide enhanced mass transfer, allowing for faster ion exchange and improved performance in high flow applications, making them ideal for more demanding water treatment scenarios.</p></p>
