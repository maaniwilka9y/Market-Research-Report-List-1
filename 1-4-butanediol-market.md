<p><strong>The 1, 4-Butanediol Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reppe Process</li><li>Davy Process</li><li>Butadiene Process</li><li>Propylene Oxide Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 1,4-Butanediol market comprises several production methods. The Reppe Process utilizes acetylene and formaldehyde to create butanediol, while the Davy Process relies on a combination of hydrogenation and hydrolysis from maleic anhydride. The Butadiene Process derives butanediol from butadiene, often using catalytic hydrogenation. Lastly, the Propylene Oxide Process converts propylene oxide into butanediol through a series of chemical reactions. Each method offers distinct advantages related to feedstock availability, cost, and environmental impact, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
