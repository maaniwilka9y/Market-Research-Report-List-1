<p><p>The instrumentation tubing market is divided into two main types: seamless tubing and welded tubing. Seamless tubing is manufactured without any joints, offering higher strength and reliability, making it ideal for high-pressure applications. Conversely, welded tubing is produced by joining flat steel sheets, providing a cost-effective option for various applications, though it may be less robust under extreme conditions. Both types serve critical roles in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and pharmaceuticals, ensuring efficient fluid and gas transfer.</p></p>
