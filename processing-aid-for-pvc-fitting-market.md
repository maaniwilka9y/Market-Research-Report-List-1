<p><p>Processing aids for PVC fitting markets are categorized primarily into two types: prills and powders. Prill forms are small, spherical beads that enhance processing efficiency and blend uniformly during manufacturing. They promote better flow and dispersion, reducing production time. Powder, on the other hand, is a fine granular form utilized for its excellent blending and melting properties, contributing to improved mechanical performance and surface finish of PVC fittings. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing the production process and product quality.</p></p>
