<p><p>The COVID-19 IgM and IgG rapid test kits encompass various market types, including Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT), Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA), and Neutralization Assays. RDTs provide quick results using a simple procedure, ideal for point-of-care testing. ELISA offers higher sensitivity and specificity for detecting antibodies but requires laboratory equipment and expertise. Neutralization assays assess the functional ability of antibodies to inhibit virus activity, typically used in research settings to understand immune responses and vaccine efficacy.</p></p>
